ABOUT ME

Ir. Adrian Bela Widodo, S.T.

Hello everyone !
I am a professional engineer with over six years of experience in industrial construction for buildings. I graduated with a Bachelor's Degree in Electrical Engineering from Diponegoro University and obtained my Professional Engineer certification from the Bandung Institute of Technology (ITB).

Currently, I am working at one of the largest National Construction Services Companies in Indonesia. Over the past six years, I have been responsible for handling Mechanical & Electrical Engineering Systems.

In my opinion, to be a professional engineer, one must prioritize safety, embody engineer culture, and embrace innovation. These values are crucial in ensuring the success and sustainability of engineering projects.
